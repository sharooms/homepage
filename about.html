FSEQ Lazy Serum About me MY GUMROAD SHOP

I’m a Berlin-based musician who lives for the high-wire act between raw energy and clinical logic.

When I discovered Ableton Live, it wasn't just a DAW—it was a revelation. I fell in love with its architectural depth and the infinite possibilities it offered. But as my music evolved, I found myself fighting the software instead of flowing with it. I was tired of clicking through endless menus and battling technical friction.

That’s when I started coding. I don't write tools for the sake of programming; I write them for the community. I’m a musician who wants to see other creators stay in the zone. I build scripts and Max for Live devices that bridge the gap between a wild idea and a finished track, ensuring you reach your creative results as fast and intuitively as humanly possible.

My goal is to strip away the technical friction so you can focus purely on the sound. Speed is everything. Creativity is everything. I code so we can all spend less time clicking and more time creating.
